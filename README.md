# 🎣 Fwish

A cozy fishing idle game. Tap to cast, mash to reel in, try not to lose the legendary ones.

## How to play

- Tap the water to cast your line
- Mash to fill the bar before the fish escapes
- Sell fish, buy upgrades, unlock new locations
- Once you buy Auto Speed, autofish runs in the background even when the tab is closed
- Charms drop occasionally and give passive bonuses
- Prestige when you're ready to start over with permanent bonuses

## Features

- 35+ fish species across 6 locations
- 15 weather types including some weird ones
- Mutations, size tiers, charm system, enchantments
- Offline progress — autofish keeps going for up to a week while you're away
- Works on mobile and desktop
- PWA — add to home screen on Android

## Play

> **Best played** by adding to your home screen (Android: Chrome/Firefox/Brave menu → "Add to Home Screen"). Playing in a browser tab may have layout issues on mobile.
